Safety Tips

  • Seatbelt Safety: Always wear seatbelts to prevent injuries in accidents.
  • Speed Limits: Obey speed limits and adjust to road conditions.
  • Avoid Distractions: Stay focused on driving; avoid texting or phone use.
  • Don't Drink and Drive: Never drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • Pedestrian Awareness: Use crosswalks, look both ways, and be visible to drivers.
  • Cyclist Safety: Wear helmets, signal turns, and follow traffic rules.
  • Weather Caution: Slow down in adverse weather conditions for better control.
  • Vehicle Maintenance: Regularly check brakes, tires, lights, and other essentials.
  • Child Safety: Properly secure children in appropriate car seats or boosters.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Know what to do in case of an accident; call emergency services if needed.

Statistics

According to the World Health Organization, road traffic injuries are the leading cause of death among young people aged 15-29.
