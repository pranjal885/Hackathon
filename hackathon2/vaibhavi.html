HOME SAFETY

Keep emergency numbers handy by each phone
Prevent falls by installing handrails,
removing tripping hazards,
and having a falls risk assessment
Protect against fire and related
dangers by using carbon monoxide
and smoke alarms Avoid bathroom hazards
by using non-slip mats,
grab bars, and shower seats
Prevent poisoning by storing medications and
chemicals safely and checking expiration dates.
